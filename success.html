About Me

I am a passionate and detail-oriented Public Health professional with over five years of experience at the intersection of research, community engagement, and program support. I specialize in bridging the gap between complex data and real-world health challenges, focusing particularly on Neglected Tropical Diseases (NTDs), nutrition, and maternal & child health. My structured and human-centered process involves synthesizing academic literature (PubMed, Google Scholar) alongside mixed-methods field research (surveys, interviews) conducted directly within communities. Beyond data collection, I am highly skilled in program support and on-site implementation, coordinating projects, managing timelines (Trello, Asana), and ensuring continuous monitoring for maximum impact. As a Virtual Research Assistant, I leverage full proficiency with digital collaboration tools (Zoom, Slack, Teams) to seamlessly translate strategic plans into successful, lasting positive health outcomes.
